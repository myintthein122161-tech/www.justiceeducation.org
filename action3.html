Our Action on Children









We are working to support children affected by the impacts of war. They urgently need Emergency assistance and international protection. That is why we are seeking safe living conditions and better educational opportunities for them in a peaceful environment.







On the other hand, we need more human resources to help these children. Although we are providing as much support as we can, there is still a great need for additional assistance.

If you are unable to provide physical help, you can still support us with even a small financial contribution. Also, if you have any suggestions, please feel free to contact us through E-text.












Together for Justice, Together for Education

We cannot end wars overnight. But we can give children the tools to rise above them. Justice Education is more than an organization-it is a movement of people who believe in fairness, compassion, and the power of knowledge.
